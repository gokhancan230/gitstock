# Price Pattern Recognition Guide

## Overview

Price patterns are recurring price structures that **predict directional moves** with higher-than-random accuracy. Use them to:
1. **Identify reversals** - When trend is ending
2. **Identify continuation** - When trend is accelerating
3. **Set profit targets** - Measured move objectives
4. **Place stops** - Pattern breakdown = wrong

---

## REVERSAL PATTERNS (Trend-Ending Signals)

### 1. HEAD & SHOULDERS ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**What It Is**:
```
       LEFT     HEAD      RIGHT
       SHOULDER  SHOULDER
         /\       /\       /\
        /  \     /  \     /  \
       /    \   /    \   /    \
      /      \ /      \ /      \
            NECKLINE
```

**Formation**:
- Left shoulder: Rally to peak, then pullback
- Head: New high above left shoulder, then pullback below left shoulder
- Right shoulder: Rally to level of left shoulder, then pullback to or below neckline
- Neckline: Support line connecting the two shoulder lows

**Measurement**:
- Target = Neckline - (Head high - Neckline)
- Example: If Head is at $150, Neckline at $140, then Target = $130

**Accuracy**: ~70% of H&S patterns complete their measured move

**Volume Profile**:
- Left shoulder: High volume
- Head: Lower volume (warning sign)
- Right shoulder: Much lower volume (confirms weakness)
- Breakout: Heavy volume confirms break

**Time to Formation**: 4-8 weeks (varies by timeframe)

**Win Rate**: 65-75% complete to target

---

### 2. DOUBLE TOP ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**What It Is**:
```
        PEAK 1        PEAK 2
         /\            /\
        /  \          /  \
       /    \        /    \
      /      \      /      \
            VALLEY
           (Support)
```

**Formation**:
- Price rises to peak, pulls back to support level
- Price rises again to approximately same level (peak 2)
- Price can exceed peak 1 slightly but not decisively
- Then breaks support line

**Measurement**:
- Drop distance from peaks to support
- Target = Support - (Peak - Support)
- Example: Peak at $100, Support at $90, Target = $80

**Accuracy**: ~65% complete measured move

**Volume Profile**:
- Peak 1: High volume
- Valley: Support holds on lower volume
- Peak 2: MUCH lower volume (key signal - weaker rally)
- Breakout down: Heavy volume

**Key Signal**: Peak 2 on lower volume = exhaustion, reversal coming

**Win Rate**: 60-70%

---

### 3. DOUBLE BOTTOM ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**What It Is** (Inverse of double top):
```
            PEAK
            /\
           /  \
          /    \
         /      \
        TROUGH 1    TROUGH 2
        /\            /\
       /  \          /  \
```

**Formation**:
- Price falls to trough, bounces to resistance
- Price falls again to approximately same level (trough 2)
- Troughs should be roughly equal (within 2-3%)
- Then breaks resistance upward

**Measurement**:
- Target = Resistance + (Resistance - Trough)
- Example: Trough at $50, Resistance at $60, Target = $70

**Volume Profile**:
- Trough 1: Very high volume (capitulation)
- Bounce: Lower volume (weak recovery)
- Trough 2: Lower volume than Trough 1 (shows less selling pressure)
- Breakout up: Heavy volume confirms

**Key Signal**: Trough 2 on lower volume = capitulation over, reversal coming

**Win Rate**: 60-70%

---

### 4. WEDGE PATTERNS ⭐⭐⭐

**Falling Wedge** (Reversal from downtrend):
```
     Peak 1
      \
       \  Peak 2
        \
         \   Peak 3
          \_______
          Support stays
```

**Formation**:
- Lower highs with each bounce (bearish)
- Support line ALSO sloping down (bullish)
- Two lines converge toward a point
- Typically lasts 4-8 weeks

**Signal**: When price breaks UP from falling wedge = REVERSAL to uptrend

**Rising Wedge** (Reversal from uptrend):
- Higher lows with each pullback (bullish)
- Resistance line ALSO sloping up even more (bearish)
- Two lines converge
- When price breaks DOWN = REVERSAL to downtrend

**Accuracy**: ~65% accuracy on breakout direction

## CONTINUATION PATTERNS (Trend-Accelerating Signals)

### 5. TRIANGLE BREAKOUT ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**What It Is**:
```
RESISTANCE
    |
    |\
    | \
    |  \
    |   \___
SUPPORT
```

**Formation**:
- Price consolidating in narrowing range
- Two converging trendlines (resistance and support)
- Volume declining as consolidation tightens
- Triangles typically last 4-12 weeks

**Types**:
- **Symmetric Triangle**: Both lines converging at same angle = Neutral bias
- **Ascending Triangle**: Support flat, resistance rising = Bullish bias
- **Descending Triangle**: Resistance flat, support falling = Bearish bias

**Breakout Signal**:
- Volume MUST expand on breakout
- Measured move target = Triangle height projected from breakout point
- Example: Triangle height $20, breakout at $100, target = $120

**Accuracy**: ~70% hit measured move target

**Win Rate**: 65-75%

---

### 6. ENGULFING CANDLE REVERSAL ⭐⭐⭐

**Bullish Engulfing**:
```
Day 1: Small red candle (down)
Day 2: Large green candle that completely encompasses Day 1
       (opens below Day 1 low, closes above Day 1 high)
```

**Setup**:
- Occurs at end of downtrend or major support
- Day 1 is usually small/declining volume
- Day 2 has much higher volume (key signal)

**Implication**: Selling exhaustion, buyers taking control

**Bearish Engulfing** (Opposite):
- Small green candle (up)
- Followed by large red candle that engulfs it
- High volume on engulfing day

**Accuracy**: ~65% when at support/major level with volume

## PATTERN SCORING & RELIABILITY

### How to Rank Pattern Strength

```
Pattern Strength Score (1-10):

Score Component:
- Formation Clarity (is pattern textbook perfect?): 0-3 points
  * 3: Textbook formation
  * 2: Mostly correct, minor variations
  * 1: Rough formation, barely meets criteria
  
- Volume Confirmation: 0-3 points
  * 3: Perfect volume (high breakout, low consolidation)
  * 2: Reasonable volume (mostly aligned)
  * 1: Poor volume (no clear volume pattern)
  
- Historical Accuracy: 0-2 points
  * 2: Pattern has proven reliable (>70% success)
  * 1: Mixed history or untested
  
- Timeframe Alignment: 0-2 points
  * 2: Pattern on Daily + Weekly aligned
  * 1: Only on Daily or only on 1H (weaker)

TOTAL PATTERN SCORE: Add all points (max 10)

Interpretation:
- 8-10: Excellent setup (high confidence)
- 6-8: Good setup (medium confidence)
- 4-6: Fair setup (moderate risk)
- <4: Poor setup (avoid or tight stop)
```

## Pattern Entry & Exit Framework

### Entry Rules

1. **Wait for Breakout Confirmation**
   - Don't enter in middle of pattern formation
   - Wait for close outside the pattern boundary
   - Volume must expand on breakout

2. **Confirm with Timeframe**
   - Pattern on Daily = Use 4H for entry confirmation
   - Pattern on 4H = Use 1H for entry confirmation
   - Don't enter on pattern formation alone

3. **Risk/Reward Check**
   - Target = Measured move
   - Risk = Stop loss below pattern breakdown
   - Ensure R/R ≥ 1:1.5

### Stop Loss Placement

**Rule**: Stop below (for bullish) or above (for bearish) the pattern breakdown level

**Examples**:
- Head & Shoulders: Stop just above right shoulder high
- Double Bottom: Stop just below the trough level
- Triangle: Stop just outside triangle boundary

**Distance**: Typically 2-3% below/above support/resistance

### Profit Taking

**Target 1**: Measured move target (as calculated for pattern)

**Target 2**: Previous major resistance/support level (or 150% of measured move)

**Trailing Stop**: Once price reaches Target 1, move stop to break-even, then trail stop

## Common Pattern Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Mistake 1: Entering Before Breakout
**Problem**: "I see the pattern forming, let me enter early"
**Result**: Pattern fails, you get stopped out before breakout even happens
**Fix**: Wait for close outside boundary with volume confirmation

### ❌ Mistake 2: Ignoring Volume on Breakout
**Problem**: Pattern breakout on very low volume
**Result**: Fake breakout, price reverses into pattern
**Fix**: Volume must expand significantly on breakout (1.5-2x average)

### ❌ Mistake 3: Wrong Measured Move Calculation
**Problem**: Miscalculating target distance
**Result**: Taking profits too early or holding too long
**Fix**: Double-check math: (Support - Resistance distance) × measured move

### ❌ Mistake 4: Pattern on Wrong Timeframe
**Problem**: Trading 1H Head & Shoulders when 4H/Daily trend is opposite
**Result**: Pattern works but larger timeframe works against you
**Fix**: Confirm pattern direction with higher timeframe trend

### ❌ Mistake 5: Overlapping Patterns
**Problem**: Multiple patterns forming simultaneously (confusing signals)
**Result**: Unclear stop placement and target
**Fix**: Focus on clearest, most reliable pattern; ignore noise

## Pattern Recognition Checklist

Before entering any pattern-based trade:

- [ ] Is pattern formation TEXTBOOK clear?
- [ ] Is volume confirming the pattern?
- [ ] Has breakout occurred with volume expansion?
- [ ] Does higher timeframe confirm direction?
- [ ] Can I calculate exact measured move target?
- [ ] Can I place logical stop loss just outside pattern?
- [ ] Is Risk/Reward minimum 1:1.5?
- [ ] Have I waited for lower timeframe confirmation?
- [ ] Is this pattern on multiple timeframes (more reliable)?
- [ ] Have I checked if similar pattern worked in past?
